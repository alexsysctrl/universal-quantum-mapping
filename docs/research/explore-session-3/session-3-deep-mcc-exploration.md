**Definition 2.1 (q-deformed Clifford algebra):**

The q-deformation changes the multiplication rules but not the dimension.

**Corrected Definition 2.4 (q-deformed Clifford algebra as a quotient of the quantum group):**
The q-deformed Clifford algebra Cl_q(p,q) is the quotient of the quantum group U_q(so(p,q)) by the ideal generated by:
  eᵢ² - gᵢᵢ = 0

where eᵢ are the generators of U_q(so(p,q)) (in the vector representation).

  Δ(eᵢ² - gᵢᵢ) = Δ(eᵢ)² - gᵢᵢ(1⊗1)
