# Exploration Session 1 — Math Deep Dive

## Goal
Explore the mathematical structure of the Modular Clifford Category from first principles. Push as far as coherence holds. Document every path, every dead end, every insight.

## Method
- Start from the primitive objects (modular Clifford modules)
- Derive everything you can
- Follow every thread, even if it leads nowhere
- When you hit a dead end, document it and try a different angle
- Track entropy/coherence — when the math becomes incoherent, stop
- Document ALL equations, ALL theorems, ALL failed attempts

## What to Explore
1. The structure of modular Clifford modules themselves
2. What functors can be defined on the category?
3. What happens in different dimensions Cl(p,q)?
4. What about infinite-dimensional extensions?
5. What about non-commutative deformations?
6. What about higher categorical structures (2-categories, ∞-categories)?
7. What topological invariants exist?
8. What about representations? Irreducibles?
9. What about deformation theory?
10. What about dualities?
11. What about discrete versions?
12. What about stochastic extensions?
13. What about quantum group deformations?
14. What about hyperbolic, elliptic, parabolic cases?
15. What about supersymmetric extensions?
16. What about p-adic versions?
17. What about finite-field versions?
18. What about non-associative generalizations?
19. What about octonionic extensions?
20. What about exceptional Lie algebra connections?

## Output
- Every equation derived (numbered)
- Every theorem proved (or attempted)
- Every dead end documented
- Every connection found
- A coherence score at each step
- A final map of what was explored and what remains
